## 💼 About Me

I'm a Software Engineer driven by a single principle: **freedom through engineering**.

I don't just build systems — I design infrastructure that removes constraints. 
Whether it's scaling bottlenecks, rigid architectures, or inefficient workflows, I aim to break them down and replace them with systems that enable speed, autonomy, and control.

My focus lies in **Platform Engineering** and **Low-Level Systems**, where I build high-performance backbones using Go and Rust — systems that are predictable, efficient, and fully under control.

I believe developers should not be limited by their tools or environments. 
That's why I invest deeply in **observability, system design, and internal platforms** — to create ecosystems where teams can move fast without losing reliability.

Currently evolving into a **Technical Architect**, with a focus on designing systems that are:
- **Unrestricted** in scalability
- **Transparent** in behavior
- **Resilient** under pressure
